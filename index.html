Property check options

Choose the visit level that fits your property.

Drive-By Property Check

A basic exterior observation from the driveway or accessible roadway.

  • General visible structure observation
  • Accessible-location photos
  • Notice of obvious damage or unusual activity
Common choice

Exterior Walk-Around Check

A closer visual review of accessible exterior areas around buildings.

  • Doors, windows, siding, and roofline observations
  • Drainage, limbs, debris, and access concerns
  • Multiple photos and written summary

Comprehensive Property Check

A more detailed visit tailored to your property and instructions.

  • Exterior check plus authorized interior walkthrough
  • Visible heating, lighting, utility, or water concerns
  • Customized documentation and prompt concern notices

Property monitoring is a visual observation service and is not a substitute for a licensed building, home, electrical, plumbing, or structural inspection.
